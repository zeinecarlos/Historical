Scientific Curiosities

The Sun is Bigger than Earth

The Sun is so large that more than one million Earths could fit inside it.

The Brain is a Supercomputer

The human brain is capable of performing billions of calculations per second, faster than any supercomputer.

Human DNA is 99.9% Identical

All humans share 99.9% of the same DNA, making us genetically almost identical.

The Universe is Expanding

Every second, the universe expands, and galaxies are moving further apart from each other.

The Lungs Produce Secondary Organs

The lungs, besides breathing, are capable of creating cells that help cleanse the body.

Water is Most Dense at 4°C

Water reaches its highest density at 4°C, which explains the strange behavior of water in ice.

Ants Can Carry 50 Times Their Weight

Some species of ants can carry objects that weigh up to 50 times their own weight.

Sharks are Older than Trees

Sharks existed over 400 million years ago, long before trees appeared on Earth.

The Moon is Moving Away from Earth

The Moon is moving away from Earth at a rate of about 3.8 cm per year.

There Are More Stars than Grains of Sand

There are more stars in the universe than all the grains of sand on Earth.
